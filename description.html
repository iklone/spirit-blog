About

This blog is dedicated to the participation in digital otaku culture. I post about my interests which are anime, manga and the otaku subculture in general.

I am a web developer by trade, and so expect some experimental web-apps along with more traditional text posts.

The admin of this website is iklone. I am an English otaku and blogger. My favourite tropes are androids, maids and mechs. My favourite series is Mahoromatic. I've run half a dozen blogs in the past and I was the president of Nottingham anime society last year. To contact me send see my contact page.
